Financing

The project can be financed by organizations, such as "Redd Barna" and funds from local governments and school administrations. Since this is a solution that should be relatively affordable to operate, the financing is planned to be achievable. Private givers are an important source of contribution for "Redd Barna", so it is important to inform and engage this group. "Redd Barna" has already many valuable partners, project partners, campaign partners and corporate sponsors which can be excellent contributors.

Fundraising to gather economical contribution on the same level that "Redd Barna" already does, engage primary and secondary schools, as well as sports clubs to arrange fundraisers and highlight the theme, and campaign driven fundraisers on social media are also valuable solutions to achieve the financing.
